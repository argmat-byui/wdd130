Córdoba

Córdoba is a province in the center of Argentina. It's bordered by Catamarca and Santiago del Estero to the north, San Luis and La Rioja to the west, Santa Fe and Buenos Aires to the east, and La Pampa to the south. It has really nice places to visit, and the oldest university in the country.
