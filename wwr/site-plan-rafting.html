Rafting Site Plan

Matías Argañaraz

Overview

Purpose

To showcase the exciting rafting experiences offered by the business, highlighting the thrill of rafting on different rivers and the expertise of the guides.

Audience

- Adventure Enthusiasts:
Age: 18-40
Financial Situation: Middle to upper-middle class
Interests: Thrill-seeking, outdoor activities, exploring nature
Lifestyle: Active, adventurous, seeking new experiences

- Students and Young Adults: Age: 18-30
Financial Situation: Limited budget
Lifestyle: Eager to explore, looking for affordable adventures
Interests: Adventure, budget-friendly travel, social experiences

- Travelers and Tourists:
Age: 25-55
Financial Situation: Middle to upper-middle class
Interests: Adventure travel, exploring new destinations
Lifestyle: Travel enthusiasts, seeking authentic experiences


1. What do they want?
Adventure enthusiasts want thrilling and memorable experiences.
Travelers and tourists want unique and authentic experiences that showcase the beauty of the destination.
Students and young adults want affordable and exciting adventures that fit their budget.

2. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?
Information about the different rafting trips available, including difficulty levels, duration, and locations.
Details about safety measures, equipment provided, and the expertise of the guides.
Pricing and booking information, including any discounts or special packages.
Customer reviews and testimonials to build trust and confidence in the services offered.
Contact information for inquiries and booking purposes.

3. How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)?
The target audience is likely to access the website through a variety of devices, including desktops, laptops, and mobile devices.
Mobile devices are particularly important, as many travelers and young adults rely heavily on their smartphones for travel planning and booking.
